Scanner & measurement settings

At the default 1:15 ratio and the 4.5 m distance used by "Place automatically", each pyrometer measures a spot of about 300 mm (30 cm). A finer ratio — e.g. changing the pyrometers to achieve a 20 cm spot — would let more of the measured surface fall under the Brick threshold, potentially pushing the "smaller than Brick" percentage toward 100%.
